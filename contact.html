Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes, all our makeup products are 100% cruelty-free and many are vegan. Our jewelry and clothing items are ethically sourced and produced.

Do you offer international shipping?

We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please contact us for specific information about shipping to your country.

How do I care for my gothic jewelry?

To maintain the dark beauty of your jewelry, store pieces separately to prevent scratching, avoid exposure to harsh chemicals, and clean gently with a soft cloth. For silver pieces, use a silver polishing cloth to maintain their luster.
